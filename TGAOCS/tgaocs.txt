--THE GOLDEN AGE OF COMPUTER SCIENCE--

--INTRODUCTION--

This essay covers a series of events from 1940-2000. It covers each decade individually and its impact on modern computers.


--1940's--

1940 - The Z2 computer is built by Konrad Zuse. It was a mechanical, relay-based machine that replaced the arithmetic logic from the original Z1 (which Zuse had built in his parent's home) with 600 relay circuits, weighing over 600lbs. (~272kgs.)
